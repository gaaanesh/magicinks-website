F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

All of the products on the website can be imprinted with the client's logo or artwork for an extra branding fee. The colour and size of the marking is determined by the product's material, dimensions, and quantity. We use digital UV printing, silk screen printing, laser engraving, embossing, and other branding processes. To learn more about each technique, click here.

Standard wrapping paper can be used to wrap gifts (charges depending on product).

The minimum order quantity varies depending on the product. The approximate MOQ is stated in each product's description, however we can accept smaller orders as well. Quantity affects both product and branding costs; the higher the quantity, the lower the price.

Almost all of the products on the website are either in stock or can be manufactured quickly. Printing and dispatching items usually takes 5-7 days. Few goods in the EXPRESS DELIVERY range can be sent with branding in as little as two working days. If an item is not readily accessible, the Magicinks sales team can confirm stock availability OR lead time if some item is not readily available.

Our rates vary depending on the quantity ordered, thus the discount will be more for bulk orders. For high-value orders, we may also provide complimentary logo branding and delivery.
